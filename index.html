What is an Emficionado?

An Emficionado is a passionate employee who combines their affinity for work with their deepest passions. It's the perfect blend of 'employee' and 'aficionado', focusing on the two things that truly matter: working for someone while pursuing your passion.

Emficionado is more than just a word - it's a philosophy that bridges the gap between personal interests and professional growth.

Our Philosophy

  • Work on whatever piques your interest and what you love most at any given time
  • Minimize obligations while maximizing payment opportunities
  • Automatically share your work with people and companies that can benefit from it
  • Send requests for payment (RFP) to give companies the opportunity to reward your work

Roadmap

  1. Create an agent to track and comprehend GitHub activities
  2. Build a network of people with shared passions through continuous search agents
  3. Establish connections and share interesting work with potential collaborators
